Our Story

Our journey began in a climbing gym. From that first meeting, we both felt an instant special connection.

What started as a climbing partnership grew into a deep friendship; before long we were falling for each other. Moments after making our feelings known, Greg took an awkward tumble while climbing, leading to our “first date” over a takeaway at A&E, and a first kiss just after midnight.

Since then, we've become a team in every way and found an unexpected depth of love. Life is simply better with the perfect companion!

During a trip to Barcelona, Greg asked Christine to marry him—she said yes!

We've had many adventures. Some, much like life, didn't go exactly to plan but proved we could handle anything together.

We're looking forward to our biggest adventure yet—marriage!
